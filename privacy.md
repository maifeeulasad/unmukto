# Privacy Policy for Unmukto

**Effective Date:** February 7, 2021

**Unmukto** (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is an open-source Bengali keyboard application available at  
https://github.com/maifeeulasad/unmukto

Unmukto is designed to work fully offline and **does not collect, transmit, or store any user data**.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

Unmukto **does not collect any personal or non-personal information** from users.

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Typed text or keystrokes
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Contacts or messages
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage or analytics data

---

## 2. No Data Storage

The app does **not store any user data** on external servers. All keyboard functionality operates locally on your device.

---

## 3. Permissions

Unmukto may request minimal system permissions required for basic keyboard functionality (such as enabling the input method).

These permissions are:
- Used **only** to provide keyboard input
- **Not** used to collect, transmit, or analyze user data

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Unmukto:
- Does **not** use third-party analytics
- Does **not** include advertising SDKs
- Does **not** integrate with tracking services

Links associated with the project (such as GitHub) may have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for third-party websites or services.

---

## 5. Security

Since Unmukto does not collect or transmit any data, there is no personal information exposed through network communication.

Users are advised to install the app only from trusted, official sources.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Unmukto does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.  
Therefore, children’s privacy is fully respected by design.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes or if required by law.  
The most recent version will always be published alongside the app or in the official repository.

---

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the maintainer via the GitHub repository:

https://github.com/maifeeulasad/unmukto
